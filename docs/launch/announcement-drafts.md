## r/islam (and r/islam_data, r/opensource)

**Title:** I built an open-source Quran reader (web + API) — free forever, contributions welcome

> As-salāmu ʿalaykum. I've been building **Ummah Library**, an open-source Quran
> reader, as a Sadaqah Jariyah. It's live and free:
>
> 🔗 https://app.ummahlibrary.org
>
> - Full Quran in Uthmani Arabic (Tanzil) with translations in English (The Clear
>   Quran), Urdu (×4), and Bengali — toggle any combination.
> - Works **offline** and installs as an app (PWA).
> - Bookmarks + continue-reading, no account or tracking.
> - A free **public API** (REST + OpenAPI + tRPC) if you want to build on the data.
>
> It's **AGPL-3.0** and owned by no one — the whole point is that it stays open.
> Code: https://github.com/UmmahLibrary/ummah-library — there are `good first
issue` tickets if you'd like to contribute (translators and developers both).
>
> Feedback and corrections are very welcome, especially on the text/translation
> rendering. JazākumAllāhu khayran.
